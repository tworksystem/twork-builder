Overview

The Jivaka Heart Centre is dedicated to providing the highest standard of cardiovascular care. Our team of expert cardiologists and cardiothoracic surgeons work together to offer a full spectrum of services, from early detection to advanced surgical interventions.

We are equipped with a state-of-the-art Cath Lab and dedicated Cardiac Operation Theatres to handle complex cases with precision. Our goal is to ensure every patient receives personalized care for a healthy heart.
