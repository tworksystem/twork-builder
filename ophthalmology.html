Eye Health Tips

20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce screen strain.

UV Protection

Always wear sunglasses that block 100% of UVA and UVB rays when outdoors.

Healthy Diet

Eat foods rich in Omega-3, Lutein, Zinc, and Vitamins C & E.

FAQ

Common Questions

How often should I get my eyes checked?
For adults, we recommend a comprehensive eye exam every 1-2 years, or annually if you have diabetes.
Is cataract surgery painful?
No, modern cataract surgery is performed under local anesthesia (eye drops) and is painless.
What is Computer Vision Syndrome?
It's eye strain from prolonged screen use. Symptoms include dry eyes, blurred vision, and headaches.

Protect Your Vision Today

Don't wait until your vision fades. Early detection is key to maintaining healthy eyes.
