Admission Process

We ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience for our patients.

1

Registration

Visit the admission counter at the lobby.

  • Bring NRC / Passport
  • Insurance Card (if any)
  • Doctor's Admission Note
  • Initial Deposit
2

During Stay

Our team will guide you to your room.

  • 24/7 Nursing Care
  • Daily Doctor Rounds
  • Dietitian Consultation
  • Room Amenities Guide
3

Discharge

Complete the process before leaving.

  • Final Bill Settlement
  • Collect Medical Reports
  • Take Home Medication
  • Follow-up Appointment

Visitor Guidelines

Do's
  • Wear a mask at all times within the premises.
  • Sanitize your hands before entering the ward.
  • Keep your mobile phones on silent mode.
  • Respect the privacy of other patients.
Don'ts
  • Do not bring outside food or flowers.
  • Children under 12 are not advised to visit.
  • Do not smoke or consume alcohol on premises.
  • Do not make loud noises in the corridors.
