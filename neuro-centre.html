Overview

The Jivaka Neuro Centre offers world-class expertise in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders. From chronic headaches to complex brain tumors and spinal injuries, we provide comprehensive care.

Our multidisciplinary team of neurologists, neurosurgeons, and rehabilitation specialists work together to deliver personalized treatment plans. We are equipped with high-precision MRI (1.5 Tesla), CT Scans, and specialized Microscopes for delicate surgeries.

Treatments & Services

We specialize in minimally invasive spine surgery and stroke management.

Stroke Management

Rapid response "Golden Hour" treatment for ischemic and hemorrhagic strokes.

Brain Tumor Surgery

Precision removal of tumors using neuro-navigation systems to protect healthy tissue.

Spinal Surgery

Treatments for slipped discs, spinal stenosis, and trauma with minimally invasive techniques.

EEG & EMG Services

Diagnostic testing for epilepsy, nerve damage, and muscle disorders.
