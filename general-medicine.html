Our General Medicine Services

We treat a wide range of acute and chronic conditions with a focus on preventative care and long-term health.

Acute Illnesses

Treatment for fevers, infections, flu, allergies, and common viral ailments requiring immediate attention.

Chronic Management

Long-term care for hypertension, diabetes, thyroid disorders, and cholesterol management.

Preventative Care

Annual health screenings, vaccinations, and lifestyle counseling to prevent future health issues.

Respiratory Care

Diagnosis and treatment for asthma, bronchitis, and other respiratory tract infections.

Gastrointestinal Health

Management of digestive disorders including gastritis, acid reflux (GERD), and irritable bowel syndrome.

Infectious Diseases

Diagnosis and treatment of seasonal flu, dengue, malaria, typhoid, and other viral or bacterial infections.

Minor Procedures

On-site care for wound dressing, stitching of minor cuts, cyst removal, and burn treatment.

Vaccinations

Routine immunizations for adults and children, including flu shots, hepatitis, and travel vaccines.

Allergy Management

Comprehensive care for seasonal allergies, skin rashes, food intolerances, and allergic reactions.

Specialized Clinics

Focused care for specific health conditions managed by our expert physicians.

Diabetes Clinic

Comprehensive sugar control and diet planning.

Hypertension Clinic

Blood pressure monitoring and heart health.

Senior Care

Geriatric medicine for elderly patients.
