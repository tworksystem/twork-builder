Common Questions

Is laparoscopic surgery painful?

It is significantly less painful than traditional open surgery. Most patients only require mild pain relievers for a few days.

How long is the hospital stay?

For most routine procedures like appendectomy or gallstone removal, patients are discharged within 24 to 48 hours.

What preparation is needed?

Generally, you will need to fast for 6-8 hours. Our team will provide a specific checklist regarding medications and hygiene before your date.
