Common Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral?
While a referral is helpful, you can also book a direct consultation with our physiotherapists for assessment.
How long is a session?
A typical session lasts between 45 to 60 minutes, depending on the treatment plan.
What should I wear?
Please wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows easy movement and access to the injured area.
