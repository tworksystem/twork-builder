Our Story

Pioneering Healthcare in Upper Myanmar

Jivaka Hospital was established with a singular vision: to provide world-class medical care with deep compassion. What started as a modest facility has now grown into Mandalay's leading multi-specialty hospital.

We believe in a holistic approach where advanced technology meets human touch. Our team of specialists works round the clock to ensure every patient receives the best possible outcome.
