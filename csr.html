Our Core Initiatives

We focus on sustainable health projects that provide long-term benefits to underprivileged communities.

Mobile Medical Clinics

Our teams travel to remote villages in Mandalay Region to provide free check-ups, basic medicines, and health education.

Charity Surgeries

We partner with local NGOs to perform free cleft lip, palate, and cataract surgeries for children and elderly in need.

Blood Donation Drives

Quarterly blood donation camps organized with our staff and public volunteers to support the National Blood Bank.
