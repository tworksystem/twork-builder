Common Questions

Everything You Need To Know

How do I know if I need hearing aids?
If you frequently ask people to repeat themselves, have trouble hearing in noisy environments, or turn up the TV volume louder than others, it's time for an assessment.
Is Endoscopic Sinus Surgery painful?
FESS is minimally invasive. Most patients experience mild discomfort rather than pain and recover within a few days.
Do you treat sleep apnea?
Yes, we offer comprehensive sleep studies and treatments ranging from CPAP therapy to surgical options for airway obstruction.
How often should I clean my ears?
Ears are self-cleaning. Avoid cotton buds. If you feel blockage, visit us for professional microsuction to avoid damage.
