FAQ

Can I drink alcohol the night before?
No, please avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours as it affects liver function tests and sugar levels.
How long does the whole process take?
A standard package typically takes about 2-3 hours. Results are usually available the same evening or next day.
Can I take my regular medication?
Yes, but please inform the doctor. For diabetes medication, do not take it before the fasting blood test.
