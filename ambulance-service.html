Common Questions

Is the service available 24/7?
Yes, our emergency response team is active 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, including public holidays.
Can I book an ambulance for non-emergencies?
Absolutely. We provide scheduled transport for patient discharge, dialysis appointments, or inter-hospital transfers.
Does insurance cover ambulance costs?
Most major health insurance policies cover emergency ambulance services. Please check with your provider or ask our billing desk.
