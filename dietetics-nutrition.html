What's Your BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple index of weight-for-height that is commonly used to classify underweight, overweight and obesity in adults.

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30 or greater

Healthy Living Tips

Simple changes for a big impact.

Hydration

Drink Before You Eat

Drinking a glass of water 30 minutes before a meal can help digestion and reduce overeating.

Snacking

Smart Snacking

Replace biscuits with nuts or fruits. They provide sustained energy instead of a sugar crash.

Sugar

Hidden Sugars

Read labels. Sauces, drinks, and 'healthy' bars often contain more sugar than you think.
